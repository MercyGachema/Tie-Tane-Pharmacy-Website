Our Services

Some of the Servics offered here include.

The common Services are as follows:

Filling prescriptions: Pharmacists dispense prescribed medications
accurately and provide appropriate dosage instructions.
Prescription transfers: Assisting patients in transferring their
prescriptions from one pharmacy to another.

Some of the Counseling services are as follows :

Medication information: Pharmacists offer comprehensive information
about medications, including usage instructions, potential side effects,
and interactions with other drugs.
Adherence support: Helping patients understand the importance of medication
adherence and providing strategies to maintain a consistent medication schedule.
Specialized medication counseling: Tailored advice for specific conditions, such as
diabetes management or anticoagulation therapy

Over The Counter Recommendations include:

Non-prescription advice: Pharmacists provide guidance on over-the-counter medications,
including suitable options for common ailments and managing minor health concerns.
Self-care recommendations: Suggesting self-care practices, lifestyle modifications, and
non-pharmacological treatments for various health conditions

Here's an overview of Immunizations and Vaccinations:

Vaccinations: Pharmacies may offer immunizations such as flu shots, tetanus
